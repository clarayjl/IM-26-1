08 Key Insights — Who We Are as a Class
📚 We're here to learn the fundamentals Almost everyone — regardless of major — wrote some version of "I want to understand how management/companies work." The class is not looking for advanced theory. Concept-first teaching with real-world examples will serve us best.
🌍 Wide range of English proficiency 33% of the class self-rated at 1 or 2 out of 5. Many international students mentioned English as a challenge. As classmates, let's be patient and inclusive — everyone is here to grow. As a class, pairing explanation with clear examples helps everyone.
💼 Most of us have real work experience 74% have worked in some capacity — hotel front desk, HR internships, campus TA roles, club leadership, part-time jobs, and more. We have a lot of real-life examples to draw from in class discussions.
🎯 Career goals are diverse — and many are still exploring From startup founders to graphic designers to NGO workers to airline crew — the range is wide. About 20% are still figuring out their direction. That's perfectly okay, and this course is a great space to explore.
🙏 We each carry something personal into this class Students shared prayer requests including senior burnout, health concerns, family situations, finding a new major, and academic pressure. Let's support one another and make this class a place of encouragement.
